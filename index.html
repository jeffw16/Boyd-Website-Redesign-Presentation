Thank you all for coming today! My name is Jeffrey Wang, and I am the project manager of the Boyd Website Redesign project.

Our current website

For the past 10 years, McKinney Boyd High School has had the same website, dedicated to providing students, parents, and teachers access to information and resources. Raise your hand if you’ve used it.

Over time, this website has become outdated and slow. As part of the 10 year anniversary, we have decided that it’s time to completely overhaul the website and rebuild it from scratch, improving the speed, quality, and design.

We’d love to show it to you now.

Video time!

It is now my pleasure to introduce Andrew Oakes, our Lead Designer.

So… here it is!

It is with great pride that the new website was built completely by Computer Science 3 students. We designed and built the website with modern standards, and the usability that comes with it. My goal was to combine elegant designs with maximum usability. A fatal flaw of our old website was the lack of any optimization for mobile devicecs, however now, our website is responsive to your device’s screen size.

We used contrasting colors on the Bronco color palate and intuitive organization to showcase important elements on the page, so that the users can easily locate each feature our development team has implemented.

For example, all of our resources have been combined into one central location for students, teachers, and parents to access when they need to, and we’ve added “Quick Links” to the navigation bar so you can access things like the APG, Canvas, HAC, Destiny, Naviance, and of course, the McKinney ISD website.

My favorite design idea was brought about by the diffuctly many parents had with the current faculty and staff pages. Our lead designer Justin Potts will later show you how we completely renovated how users look for teachers.

New faculty directory

(Thanks Jeffrey Wang, Andrew Oakes, Scott McFarlane, Marvin Fuxa!)

One of my favorite new features to the site is the addition of searching for faculty. No more looking through an endless list of names you don’t recognize, until you find your teacher, only to have trouble reading their information. We’ve completely redesigned the process to make it easier for you to find the information you need.

As you saw in the video, if I type in a search query, it immediately suggests teachers' names, even if it's a partial match. Then, if I click on their name, it jumps directly to all of their information.

Senior info

It's important to put information on the new Boyd website in a centralized place for our major events. For example, you can now use our "Senior info" page to check out the details about prom, graduation, and other senior events on the senior calendar.
